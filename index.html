Olivia Harrison

Archivist & Artist

Olivia Harrison is a young artist living and working in New York. Her work touches on the subtleties of
every day life while simultaneously trying to grasp the scope of who and where we are as human beings on a planet.

This is a collection of the things I encounter everyday and a place to share things that I find important.
